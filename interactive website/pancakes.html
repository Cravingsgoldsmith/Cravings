Pancakes recipe

Serving: 12-14

Total time: 1hr 5min

Prep time: 18min

Cooking time: 15min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 12 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter or cooking oil

Method

Step 1:

Heat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat.

Step 2:

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

Step 3:

In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, milk, egg, and melted butter.

Step 4:

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. It's okay if there are a few lumps; overmixing can result in tough pancakes. .

Step 5:

Lightly grease the griddle or pan with cooking spray or a small amount of butter.

Step 6:

Pour 1/4 cup portions of batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake and the edges look set. Flip and Cook the Other Side: Carefully flip the pancakes with a spatula and cook the other side until golden brown.

Step 7:

Continue making pancakes with the remaining batter. Add more butter or cooking spray to the pan as needed.
