Fried Rice Recipe

Serving: 2

Total time: 20 mins

Prep time: 5 mins

Cooking time: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs, 1 beaten
  • bunch of spring onions, chopped into 3cm lengths, tops finely sliced to serve
  • 1 green chilli, chopped
  • 2cm chunk of ginger, grated
  • 1 garlic clove, grated
  • 150g spring greens, shredded
  • 250g pouch of ready-cooked rice
  • 1 ½ tbsp soy sauce, plus extra to taste
  • sesame seeds, toasted, to serve

Method

Step 1:

Heat a splash of the oil in a large non-stick frying pan over a high heat. When hot, crack the eggs in and turn the heat down.

Step 2:

This should make the eggs nice and crispy – without burning – while the yolks cook. Use a fish slice to remove, set aside on kitchen paper and cover with foil to keep warm.

Step 3:

Add the remaining oil to the pan, then scatter in the chopped spring onions, chilli, ginger and garlic and gently fry until softened, about 2-3 mins.

Step 4:

Tip in the beaten egg and leave for 30 seconds until just set, then mash up with a spoon.

Step 5:

Tip in the spring greens with a splash of water and cook until wilted. Add the rice and soy sauce and mix everything together, then season and tip into two bowls.
